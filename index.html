Do you know

cleanser

A facial cleanser is a skincare product used to remove all types of pollutants from the skin, and prevent skin conditions such as acne.

Sunscreen

Sunscreens protect the skin. They play an important role in blocking ultraviolet (UV) radiation from being absorbed by the skin.

Moisturizer

It is an important product to maintain skin health as well as treating various dermatoses which co-exist with skin dryness.

shampoo

shampoo not only provides the cleaning of the scalp skin and hair as its primary function, but it also acts as an adjunct in the management of various scalp disorders.

Conditioner

Conditioner is the cornerstone of any good hair care routine. After you cleanse your hair with shampoo, you need to add a bit of moisture back in to fortify and smooth your follicles.

Hair serum

Hair serum is a liquid-based treatment usually formulated using silicones that provide a protective layer over the hair fibre, assisting in smoothing frizz.
